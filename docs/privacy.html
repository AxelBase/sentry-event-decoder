Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2025 • Oslo, Norway

1. Our Core Principle: Zero Data Collection

Sentry Event Decoder is a fully client-side, open-source Progressive Web App developed by AxelBase. We believe privacy is non-negotiable. That’s why we collect absolutely no personal data, usage analytics, or event content — ever.

2. What We Do Not Collect

Your Sentry event JSON, trace data, or any input is processed exclusively in your browser. We do not collect:

  • Personal information: No names, emails, or identifiers.
  • Usage data: No IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or session tracking.
  • Event or trace content: Your JSON never leaves your device.
  • Analytics or cookies: No Google Analytics, Plausible, or any tracking scripts.

3. How the Tool Works

All parsing, visualization (vis-network), and export features (html2canvas, FileSaver.js) run locally. Even when you paste a public Sentry event URL, the fetch happens from your browser only — we never see it. The service worker caches assets for offline use, but never user data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry services. All dependencies are bundled locally or loaded from trusted CDNs without tracking.

5. Data Security & Your Responsibility

Since no data is transmitted, we cannot be breached. However, if your event JSON contains sensitive information (e.g., tokens, PII), it remains only in your browser’s memory and local cache. We recommend clearing your browser cache after use if handling confidential traces.

6. Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Compliance

By design, we comply with GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, and all major privacy laws — because we collect nothing.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the new effective date.

9. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub: github.com/AxelBase/sentry-event-decoder

Your traces are yours. We see nothing. We store nothing. Privacy by design — from Oslo, Norway, with integrity.
