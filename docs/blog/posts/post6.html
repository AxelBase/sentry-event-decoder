Export Trace as PNG/JSON

Export Trace as PNG or JSON – Share with Your Team

Published: November 17, 2025

You found the problem. Now you need to show it to your team. Two buttons in Sentry Event Decoder make that instant:

  • Export PNG → Perfect screenshot of exactly what you see (powered by html2canvas)
  • Export JSON → Full trace context for scripting or archiving

Use Cases That Save Hours

  • Drop a PNG in Slack: “Here’s why checkout is slow”
  • Attach to a Jira ticket with zero explanation needed
  • Paste into Notion runbooks
  • Include in incident post-mortems

No More “Just Click This Link”

Links to Sentry traces break, require login, or load slowly. A PNG is universal, instant, and unambiguous.

FAQ

Does the PNG include the zoomed view?

Yes — whatever you see on screen is exactly what gets exported.

Is the JSON export the same as Sentry’s?

It’s the cleaned trace context — perfect for diffs or automation.

Great debugging isn’t just finding the problem — it’s communicating it clearly. Export buttons make that effortless.
